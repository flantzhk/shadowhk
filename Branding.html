Brand thesis

Learning Cantonese is learning Hong Kong.

The city, its people, its streets, its food — inseparable from the language. Every screen should feel like a page from a magazine that loves Hong Kong. Adult, sophisticated, never childlike, never cute.

01PrinciplesFour rules

How the brand behaves

Four rules that govern every design decision. When the editorial instinct and the gamified instinct fight, the editorial side wins.

No. 01

Cinema, not stock.

Photography is the brand. Shot like a Wong Kar-wai film — dense, saturated, half-seen. Vermilion bleeds, jade silk in shadow, amber lamp glow. Subjects framed by doorways, mirrors, rain on glass. Always 21:9 letterbox, always with grain.

No. 02

The chrome stays light.

The app itself sits in warm off-white. Cinematic darkness lives only inside photographs and hero plinths — the surfaces around them breathe. No dark mode in v1. Silence and margin are part of the design.

No. 03

Vermilion is the only accent.

One red carries every CTA, every score, every active state. Sage is for context and mastery. Ink and oxblood live in photography. Never rainbow, never neon glow.

No. 04

Celebration with restraint.

Reward moments use editorial typography and tasteful confetti — vermilion + sage + warm gold only. The user should feel proud, not patronised.

02Colour12 tokens · 3 groups

04PhotographyWong Kar-wai · cinematic

Shot like 2046.

Photography references late-period Wong Kar-wai — 2046, In the Mood for Love, Chungking Express. Dense, saturated, cinematic. A figure half-seen through smoke or rain on glass. Vermilion bleeds, jade silk in shadow, amber lamp glow, ink-teal blacks. Letterboxed crops. Subtle film grain on every image. The frame holds memory, not documentation.

The look — what to brief a photographer
Colour
Cross-processed feel. Reds saturated to bleeding. Greens pulled to forest/jade. Skin warm amber. Blacks read as ink-teal or oxblood, never neutral.
Light
Practical sources only — neon, lamp, fluorescent strip, candle. Never flash. Highlights blown gently to gold; shadows lifted slightly to magenta or teal.
Frame
Subjects off-centre. Foreground obstruction encouraged: doorways, mirrors, rain on glass, train windows, smoke, hanging meat, beaded curtains. The viewer is always looking through something.
Texture
16mm/35mm film grain present on every shot. No clean digital. Slight defocus on edges of frame. 21:9 letterbox where the layout allows.

08MotionPulse, pop, glow

Reward, not pageantry.

Every animation is short, warm, and confined. We pulse, we pop, we glow — we never bounce. Confetti is restrained: vermilion, sage, gold. Always premium magazine, never children's game.

Mic pulse

Vermilion circle pulses 1.6s ease-in-out. Signals always-on listening.

Score pop

Scale 0 → 1.2 → 1.0 over 250ms ease. Used for +10s, percentages, streaks.
